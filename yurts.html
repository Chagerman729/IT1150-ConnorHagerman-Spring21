The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent strucutures four feet off the ground.
Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas calls, a wooden floor, and a
roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yruts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed iwth down quilt and
gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience inlcudes electricity
and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom
facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for
changing weahter with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to
bring a flashlight and a sense fo adventure!
